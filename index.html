Story

The story is about campus students and the influence of peer pressure. Two of the students (Rahel Dires and Meti Terefe) are addicted and they are pressuring their dormmate (Hermela Mekonnen) to join them on their addiction. Hermela is a clever student, who usually spend her time at the library studying but their pressure and the difficulty of exams and other circumstances made her join them but after a while a proctor (Naima Tilahun) caught them in the act and they were expelled from school. So, they rented a house outside campus so that their parents wouldn’t know but this was very hard for them especially for Hermela, she couldn’t handle the stress and decided to take her own life.
